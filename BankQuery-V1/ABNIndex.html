ABNUNISOL BANK SERVICE v2.0.4
ABNUnisol Bank Service
The service is used to process payments and receipt on the core ERP.
The service has two Endpoints
  1. IsThisBonaFideStudent :- Verifies whether the provided student registration number is a valid student.
  2. ProcessStudentFees :- Process the fees payment for the provided registration number-valid student/applicant.
    NOTE: A receipt number will only be provided upon successful request

The Service entry point is /ABNUnisolBankService.asmx
